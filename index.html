Notion Quote Widgets

A collection of minimalist, elegant quote generators built specifically for embedding in Notion pages.

How to use

  1. Choose your preferred quote collection from the options on the right
  2. Click "Copy embed link" below the preview
  3. In Notion, paste the link and choose embed block and paste the URL
  4. If necessary, adjust the height of the embed to fit the quote card
